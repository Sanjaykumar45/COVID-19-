Prevention from Corona Virus

stay home, stay safe

how to prevent

how to prevent virus

wash your place

Clean high touch surfaces regularly or as needed and after you have visitors in your home. If surfaces are dirty, clean them using detergent or soap and water prior to disinfection.

maintain distance

Avoid close contact with people who are sick. Stay at least 6 feet (about 2 arm lengths) from other people, especially if you are at higher risk of getting very sick.

don't touch face

To reduce the spread of coronavirus (COVID-19), experts recommend that you not touch your face because the virus is transmitted through mucous membranes in the mouth, nose and eyes.

wash your hand

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds especially after you have been in a public place, or after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing.

use napkin

Always cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze, or use the inside of your elbow and do not spit.

wear a mask

Everyone 2 years or older who is not fully vaccinated should wear a mask in public places. Covers your nose and mouth and can be secured under your chin.

things you should DO

wash your hand

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds especially after you have been in a public place, or after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing.

always wear a mask

Everyone always wear a mask in public places. Covers your nose and mouth and can be secured under your chin.

stay home during fever

If you have a fever, cough or other symptoms, you might have COVID-19. Do not leave your home, except to get medical care. Do not visit public areas.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Some coronaviruses that infect animals can be spread to people and then spread between people,should avoid contact with animals, including pets, livestock, and wildlife.

avoid close contact with peoples

Avoid close contact with people who are sick. Stay at least 6 feet (about 2 arm lengths) from other people, especially if you are at higher risk of getting very sick.

avoid crowded area

Being in crowded places like restaurants, bars, fitness centers, or movie theaters puts you at higher risk for COVID-19, should avoid crowded area. If indoors, bring in fresh air by opening windows and doors, if possible.
